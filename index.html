Privacy Policy

AnamorphicDesqueezer

Effective date: January 4, 2026

Overview

AnamorphicDesqueezer respects your privacy. This app is designed to desqueeze anamorphic photos and videos, including batch processing, entirely on your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app does not require user accounts or authentication.

2. Camera and Photo Library Access

The app may request access to the following:

  • Camera – to capture photos and videos with real-time anamorphic preview.
  • Photo Library (Read) – to allow selection of existing photos and videos for desqueeze.
  • Photo Library (Add) – to save exported desqueezed photos and videos back to your device.

All access is initiated by the user and used only for the app’s core functionality.

3. Batch Desqueeze Processing

The app supports batch desqueeze, allowing users to process multiple photos and/or videos in one operation. All batch processing is performed locally on the device. No media files are uploaded to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

AnamorphicDesqueezer does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or cloud services.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no data is collected, no personal information is stored for any user.

6. Changes to This Policy

If future updates introduce features that affect privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly and reflected in the App Store privacy disclosures.
